Spring In My Step

Concept

Spring In My Step is a solo project I created for the 2024 Turkey Jam. It is a Side-scroller heavily inspired by PogoStuck. It featured a player which must use their pogostick to navigate obstacles to get to the bus stop. The player must ensure not to land too awqwardly lest they fall off their pogo stick.

I hoped to experiment in this game by using only a single gradient of color for the game's visuals. To help make the game feel less flat, instead of using a normal diffuse shader I created a shader which applied the color by distance and angle with the camera to give the game a more unique look.

Controls

  • WASD/Arrow keys to rotate
  • Space to Jump higher
